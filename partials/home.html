Fool Hearts is a project almost 10 years in the making. Be it on stage, in studio, or around a kitchen table, we've amassed countless hours playing music together, and a formidable repertoire spanning decades.



We specialize in weddings and corporate events, but found our legs in clubs, bars, backyards, and barns.
